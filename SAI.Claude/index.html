Our Mission

Redefining what Israel can achieve

Israel has the talent. What it has lacked is the system. For too long, our most gifted athletes have been forced off the path to greatness — not by their rivals, but by a culture that made them choose between sport and education.

SAI exists to change that permanently. We are building the national infrastructure that will make Israel a force on the world's biggest sporting stages — by giving every elite athlete the support, the resources, and the institutional backing to compete without compromise.

This is not just about scholarships. It is about transforming what Israeli sport means — to our athletes, to our youth, and to the world watching us compete.

MOU Signed · Bar-Ilan University
The SAI Framework

Two paths. One mission.

SAI is built around two distinct tracks — each designed for where an athlete actually is in their career, not a one-size-fits-all model.

Track A

Olympic &
Paralympic Athletes

National & International Competitors

For athletes actively competing at the national team level or higher. This track is built around the demands of elite training schedules, international competition calendars, and long-term athletic careers.

✦ Academic accommodations around training and competition
✦ Dedicated SAI academic coordinator managing university liaison
✦ Dedicated tutor for catch-up sessions after travel and training blocks
✦ Scholarship support
Track B

High-Performance
Athletes

Rising Competitors & National Level

For athletes competing seriously at a high national level who are building toward elite status. This track provides structured support to develop both athletic and academic potential simultaneously.

✦ Academic accommodations around training and competition
✦ Dedicated SAI academic coordinator managing university liaison
✦ Dedicated tutor for catch-up sessions after travel and training blocks
✦ Pathway to Track A if competitive level rises

Track placement is determined in partnership with the athlete, their coach, and the SAI selection committee.
